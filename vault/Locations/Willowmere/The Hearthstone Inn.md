# The Hearthstone Inn

The only inn and tavern in [[Willowmere]], serving as the social hub for the entire village.

## Description

A sturdy two-story building of timber and stone, with a large common room on the ground floor and a handful of modest guest rooms above. A massive stone hearth dominates the main room—the inn's namesake—where a fire burns year-round, filling the space with warmth and the smell of woodsmoke.

The ceiling is low, the furniture is worn but sturdy, and the atmosphere is cozy rather than cramped. Dried herbs and smoked meats hang from the rafters, and a well-used dartboard occupies one corner.

## Proprietors

The inn is run by [[Gareth Hollowell]] and his wife [[Hanna Hollowell]].

**[[Gareth Hollowell]]** — A barrel-chested, bald man in his mid-forties with faded sailor's tattoos on his forearms. A former merchant sailor who settled in Willowmere after nearly dying in a storm at sea. Gareth runs the front of house with warmth and charm, always eager for news and stories from travelers.

**[[Hanna Hollowell]]** — A sturdy, practical woman who commands the kitchen and keeps the inn running smoothly. Born and raised in Willowmere, she's the disciplinarian of the operation. Her cooking is legendary.

Their children **Tam** (16) and **Lily** (12) help around the inn.

## Atmosphere

Evenings see the farmers gather here after a long day's work, sharing ale and gossip. Someone usually has a fiddle or a pipe, and impromptu music is common. The inn is the first place travellers are directed—and the best place to hear local news and rumours.

## Menu Specialties

- **Hearthstone Stew** — A hearty lamb and root vegetable stew, Hanna's specialty
- **Forest Honey Mead** — Made with honey harvested from the nearby woods
- **Shepherd's Pie** — A local favourite on cold nights

## Staff

### [[Lily Hollowell]]
The Hollowells' 12-year-old daughter. A quiet, bookish girl who helps clear tables, wash dishes, and assist her mother in the kitchen. Often found reading in corners when business is slow. Guests who take the time to talk to her find she notices everything that happens in the inn.
