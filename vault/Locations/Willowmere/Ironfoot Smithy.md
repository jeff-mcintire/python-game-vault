# Ironfoot Smithy

A modest blacksmith shop in [[Willowmere]], primarily serving the village's farming community.

## Description

A squat stone building with a perpetually smoking chimney, set slightly apart from the other structures to reduce fire risk. The rhythmic clang of hammer on anvil echoes through the village most mornings. The forge is open-fronted, allowing passersby to watch the smith at work.

Tools, horseshoes, and repaired farm implements hang from hooks and fill barrels outside. The smell of hot metal and coal smoke permeates the area.

## Proprietor

**[[Breta Ironfoot]]** — A dwarven woman who settled in Willowmere decades ago after leaving her clan for reasons she doesn't discuss. Stocky and strong even by dwarf standards, with arms like tree trunks and soot perpetually smudged on her face. She's gruff but fair, and takes pride in honest work. The villagers have long since stopped noticing she's a dwarf—she's just "the smith."

## Notes

- Breta can repair most metal goods, given time and materials
- She occasionally trades with merchants for ore, but supplies can be limited
- Rumour has it she was once a weaponsmith of some renown
