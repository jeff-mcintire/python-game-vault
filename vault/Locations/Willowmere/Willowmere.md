# Willowmere

A small, out-of-the-way village nestled in a secluded valley, surrounded by dense forests and towering mountains. Willowmere is a quiet, pastoral community where most residents make their living from the land.

## Description

Willowmere sits at the edge of a gentle stream, with a cluster of modest thatch-roofed cottages arranged around a central dirt road. Ancient willow trees line the water's edge, their drooping branches swaying in the breeze—giving the village its name. The surrounding mountains shelter the valley from harsh winds, creating a surprisingly mild climate for such a remote location.

The forest presses close on all sides, and the only road in or out winds through several miles of woodland before reaching any major thoroughfare. This isolation has made Willowmere a tight-knit community, where everyone knows their neighbours and strangers are a rare curiosity.

## Key Locations

- [[The Hearthstone Inn]] — The village's combined inn and tavern, the social heart of Willowmere
- [[Ironfoot Smithy]] — A modest blacksmith shop run by a skilled farrier
- [[Mabel's Sundries]] — A small general store carrying essentials

## Economy

The village economy revolves around:
- **Farming** — Wheat, barley, and root vegetables
- **Livestock** — Sheep and goats graze the hillsides
- **Forestry** — Limited logging and foraging from the surrounding woods
- **Hunting** — Game from the forest supplements the village diet

## Notable Features

- **The Old Willow** — An enormous ancient willow tree at the village centre, said to be over 500 years old
- **Mountain Springs** — Fresh, cold water flows down from the peaks into the village stream
- **The Forest Road** — The winding path through the woods, often overgrown and treacherous in poor weather

## Atmosphere

Life in Willowmere moves slowly. The villagers are simple, hardworking folk—suspicious of outsiders at first but warm and generous once trust is earned. News from the outside world arrives infrequently, and most residents prefer it that way.

## Hooks & Rumours

- Hunters have reported strange lights in the mountains at night
- An old hermit lives somewhere deep in the forest—some say she's a witch
- The road has become more dangerous lately; something is spooking the horses
