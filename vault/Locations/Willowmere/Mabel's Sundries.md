# Mabel's Sundries

A small general store in [[Willowmere]], carrying everyday essentials for the village's residents.

## Description

A cramped but cozy shop occupying the front room of a cottage near the village centre. Shelves line every wall from floor to ceiling, packed with an eclectic assortment of goods. Barrels of flour, salt, and dried beans crowd the floor space, and bundles of rope and twine hang from the ceiling.

The shop smells of dried lavender, candle wax, and old wood. A fat orange cat named **Turnip** lounges on the counter, supervising all transactions.

## Proprietors

**[[Mabel Dunbrook]]** — A plump, cheerful woman in her late fifties with grey-streaked brown hair escaping from a messy bun. Born and raised in [[Willowmere]], she's run the shop since her husband Bertram passed away ten years ago. Mabel knows everyone's business (in the kindest way) and is the village's unofficial gossip hub.

**[[Corwin Dunbrook]]** — Mabel's nephew, a charming young man from the city who arrived two years ago to help his aunt. Handsome and quick-witted, he's made the shop run more efficiently—though some wonder what really brought him to such a remote village.

## Notes

- Mabel occasionally has rare items from travelling merchants—ask what's "special" this week
- She'll buy foraged herbs, pelts, and other goods from adventurers
- The shop cat, Turnip, is an excellent judge of character (according to Mabel)
- [[Corwin Dunbrook]] handles the accounts and inventory with surprising skill
