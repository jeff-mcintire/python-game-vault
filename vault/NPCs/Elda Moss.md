# Elda Moss

*née Thorne*

> *"The forest gives us everything we need, if you know where to look."*

## Appearance

Elda is a slender woman with an earthy, practical beauty. She has long auburn hair usually braided with dried flowers or herbs, and keen green eyes that seem to notice everything. Her hands are often stained with plant pigments, and she carries the pleasant scent of dried lavender and chamomile.

She dresses in layers of homespun wool and linen, with a leather satchel always at her hip filled with remedies and herbs.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Warm and nurturing; the village turns to her in times of illness or birth
- Curious about the natural world; always learning
- More open-minded than most villagers
- Has a calming presence in crisis

**Ideals:**
- *Healing* — "Every life is precious and worth fighting for."
- *Knowledge* — There's always more to learn from the forest

**Bonds:**
- Deeply connected to her brother [[Jorin Thorne]] and his children
- Cares for her father [[Aldric Thorne]], though they sometimes clash
- Her husband [[Willem Moss]] is her partner in all things

**Flaws:**
- Sometimes ventures too deep into the forest alone
- Trusts too easily; believes the best in people

## Background

Elda always had a gift for plants and healing. As a girl, she would disappear into the woods for hours, returning with armfuls of herbs. An old woman named Granny Fetch taught her the healing arts before passing away years ago. Now Elda serves as [[Willowmere]]'s informal healer, treating everything from fevers to difficult births.

She married [[Willem Moss]], a hunter, and they built a cottage on the village outskirts where the forest meets the fields.
