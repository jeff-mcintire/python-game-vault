# Corwin Dunbrook

> *"Willowmere's quiet. That's exactly what I needed."*

## Basic Information

| | |
|---|---|
| **Race** | Human |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Age** | 24 |
| **Occupation** | Shop Assistant |
| **Location** | [[Mabel's Sundries]], [[Willowmere]] |

## Appearance

Corwin is a handsome young man with an easy smile that doesn't quite reach his eyes. He has dark hair, kept fashionably trimmed (by village standards), and brown eyes that are always watching, calculating. His hands are soft—clearly not used to farm work—and he dresses slightly better than most villagers, in clothes that hint at city origins.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Charming and quick-witted
- Helpful and hard-working (perhaps too eager to please)
- Evasive about his past
- Surprisingly well-educated for a shop assistant

**Ideals:**
- *Second Chances* — Everyone deserves one
- *Survival* — Do what you must to get by

**Bonds:**
- Genuinely cares for his aunt [[Mabel Dunbrook]]
- Is starting to appreciate the simple life of [[Willowmere]]

**Flaws:**
- Hiding from something—or someone—in his past
- Old habits of deception die hard
- Jumpy around strangers, especially well-dressed ones

## Background

Corwin arrived in [[Willowmere]] two years ago, claiming he wanted to help his aging aunt with the shop. The truth is more complicated: he fled the city after getting involved with the wrong people—a thieves' guild, a noble's angry family, gambling debts, or all three. He's genuinely trying to start fresh, but fears his past will catch up with him.

## Abilities & Skills

- **Sleight of Hand:** Old habits; he doesn't steal anymore, but the skills remain
- **Persuasion:** Naturally charming and convincing
- **Streetwise:** Knows how criminals and cities work
- **Literacy:** Can read and write, rare in the village

## Roleplaying Notes

- Friendly and helpful, perhaps overly so
- Changes the subject smoothly when his past comes up
- Nervous around anyone who seems official or wealthy
- Genuinely good with customers; the shop has done better since he arrived

## Plot Hooks

- Someone from his past arrives in [[Willowmere]] looking for him
- He might recognize criminals or thieves' cant if encountered
- Could be recruited for tasks requiring his "particular skills"
- Might confide in trustworthy adventurers if he fears danger is coming
