# Jorin Thorne

> *"There's got to be more to life than planting and harvesting, year after year."*

## Appearance

Jorin is broad-shouldered and strong, built for farmwork with rough hands and sun-darkened skin. He has his father's height but his late mother's dark brown hair, which he wears longer than practical, often pushed back from his face. His brown eyes carry a hint of restlessness, always seeming to look toward the horizon.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Hardworking but dreams of adventure
- Good-natured and quick to laugh
- Loves his children fiercely
- Feels trapped by duty and expectation

**Ideals:**
- *Freedom* — Yearns to see what lies beyond the valley
- *Family* — But would never abandon those who depend on him

**Bonds:**
- His children Pip and Rosie are his world
- Feels obligated to care for his aging father [[Aldric Thorne]]

**Flaws:**
- Sometimes resentful of his responsibilities
- Romanticizes the outside world he's never seen

## Background

Jorin grew up hearing traders' tales at [[The Hearthstone Inn]] and always dreamed of leaving [[Willowmere]] to see the world. But duty kept him—first to help his father, then marriage, then children. When his wife Senna died, any thought of leaving vanished. Now he channels his restless energy into being the best father he can.
