# Mabel Dunbrook

> *"I've got just the thing, dear. Now where did I put it..."*

## Appearance

Mabel is a plump, rosy-cheeked woman with a perpetually warm smile. Her grey-streaked brown hair is always escaping from the bun she pins it in, and her spectacles seem permanently perched on the end of her nose. She wears practical aprons over simple dresses, the pockets always stuffed with odds and ends.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Cheerful and chatty; knows everyone's business
- Disorganized but somehow always finds what's needed
- Generous to a fault; extends credit freely
- Sharp memory for gossip, terrible memory for where she put things

**Ideals:**
- *Community* — The shop is as much a gathering place as a business
- *Kindness* — "A little generosity never hurt anyone."

**Bonds:**
- [[Mabel's Sundries]] has been in her family for generations
- Her nephew [[Corwin Dunbrook]] helps run the shop
- Considers the whole village her extended family

**Flaws:**
- Too trusting; has been cheated by travelers before
- Terrible at saying no, even when she should
- Her cluttered shop is a fire hazard waiting to happen

## Background

Mabel was born in [[Willowmere]] and has never left. She married Bertram Dunbrook, who ran the sundries shop, and they never had children of their own. When Bertram passed, Mabel took over completely. Her nephew Corwin came to help a few years ago, and she's been teaching him the trade—though she suspects he has his own reasons for leaving the city.
