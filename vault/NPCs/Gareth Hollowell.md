## Appearance

Gareth is a barrel-chested man with a booming laugh and a bald head he keeps polished with pride. His thick forearms are covered in faded tattoos—remnants of a youth spent as a sailor before he settled inland. He has warm brown skin, a neatly trimmed beard going grey at the edges, and crow's feet from years of smiling.

He's always wiping down the bar or carrying trays, never idle.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Jovial and welcoming; makes everyone feel at home
- Loves a good story; collects tales from travelers
- Protective of his establishment and his village
- Surprisingly perceptive behind the friendly demeanor

**Ideals:**
- *Hospitality* — "No one leaves The Hearthstone hungry or cold."
- *Stories* — Every person who walks through the door carries a tale worth hearing

**Bonds:**
- [[The Hearthstone Inn]] is his pride and legacy
- His wife [[Hanna Hollowell]] is his partner in everything
- Feels responsible for keeping [[Willowmere]] connected to the wider world

**Flaws:**
- Tends to meddle in others' affairs "for their own good"
- Can be overprotective of his family
- His seafaring past holds some chapters he doesn't discuss

## Background

Gareth spent his youth as a sailor on merchant vessels, seeing distant ports and surviving more than a few dangers. A storm that nearly killed him made him reconsider his life. He wandered inland, found [[Willowmere]], and fell in love with both the village and a local woman named Hanna.

Together they transformed a rundown alehouse into [[The Hearthstone Inn]], the warm heart of the community.
