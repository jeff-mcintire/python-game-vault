# Willem Moss

> *"The forest has moods, same as people. Learn to read them, or it'll be the death of you."*

## Appearance

Willem is lean and wiry, built for moving silently through the underbrush. He has shaggy brown hair, a short beard, and pale grey eyes that seem to take in everything at once. His face is weathered beyond his years from long days in the elements. 

He wears practical hunting leathers, often patched and mended, and carries a well-worn longbow that was his father's before him.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Quiet and observant; more comfortable with silence than conversation
- Patient—can wait motionless for hours
- Fiercely protective of his family
- Respectful of nature; never takes more than needed

**Ideals:**
- *Balance* — "Take only what the forest can spare."
- *Vigilance* — Danger comes to the unwary

**Bonds:**
- His wife [[Elda Moss]] and daughter Tansy are everything to him
- Knows the forest better than anyone in [[Willowmere]]

**Flaws:**
- Socially awkward; struggles in groups
- Can be overprotective to the point of paranoia

## Background

Willem grew up on the edge of [[Willowmere]], orphaned young when sickness took his parents. The forest became his home and teacher. He learned to track, trap, and hunt, providing game for the village in exchange for what he needed.

When he met Elda Thorne gathering herbs, something softened in him. Their courtship was slow and quiet, but they understood each other. Now they live together in a cottage where forest meets field, raising their daughter Tansy.
